Leaders of the City

The First Family

Senior Pastor & Founder

Rev. S.G. Erochukwu

Called with a mandate to possess the land and establish the Kingdom of God in the hearts of men, Rev. S.G. Erochukwu has spent over two decades in ministry. His teachings are centered on the power of the Word and the tangible presence of the Holy Spirit.


Under his leadership, Winning City has expanded from a small fellowship into a global community of believers dedicated to kingdom expansion and spiritual transformation.

The Mother of the City

Pastor Grace Erochukwu

A woman of virtue and deep intercession, Pastor Grace is the heartbeat of the Winning City family. She leads the Women of Grace ministry and is a pillar of support to the vision of the church.


Her passion for family, parenting, and mentorship has raised thousands of young women into their God-given purposes. She believes that a strong church starts with a strong, God-fearing home.
