Introduction

Welcome to Winning City. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your spiritual journey through our digital portal remains secure and sanctified. This policy outlines how we handle the information you entrust to us.

Data Collection

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on our portal, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our services, or when you participate in activities on the site.

Personal Identifiers: Name, Email Address, Phone Number (including country data), and City of residence.

Prayer Intercession

Any information shared via our "Altar of Intercession" (Prayer Request Form) is treated with the highest level of confidentiality. This data is transmitted directly to our ministerial team for the purpose of prayer and spiritual support.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your prayer requests with third-party organizations for marketing purposes.

WLBC TV & Media

Our media portal uses high-frequency digital transmission protocols. When you interact with WLBC TV or our Audio Vault, we may collect technical data regarding your connection speed and device type to ensure the "Rhema" reaches you without interruption.

YouTube embeds and external media players follow their respective privacy protocols, which are integrated into our secure vault system.

Digital Identity (Cookies)

Winning City uses small data files known as "Cookies" to remember your preferences (like theme settings and video volume). These cookies help us provide a seamless experience as you navigate between the Sanctuary and the Media Hub.

Contact & Support
