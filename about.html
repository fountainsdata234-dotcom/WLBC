Who We Are

Discover the heart and soul of Winning City

Our Journey

Winning Life Bible Church began with a divine mandate to raise a people of righteousness. From humble beginnings, God has expanded our territory, touching lives across Aba and beyond.

Under the leadership of Rev. S.G Erochukwu, we have witnessed countless testimonies of transformation, healing, and restoration. Our history is not just about dates and buildings; it is a story of God's faithfulness.

What We Believe

The Trinity

We believe in God the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

The Word

The Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God.

Salvation

Saved by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

Resurrection

Christ rose from the dead, and so shall we.

Our Vision

To raise a people who are conscious of their Righteousness in Christ Jesus.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel of Grace, liberate men from the shackles of religion, and demonstrate the power of the Holy Spirit.
