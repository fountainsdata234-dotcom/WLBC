Do You Want To Give Your Life To Christ?

Your journey to a new life starts with these steps of faith.

Admission is the first step to healing. Recognize that we have all fallen short of God's glory and need His redeeming love to be made whole again.

“Now the works of the flesh are manifest... Adultery, fornication, uncleanness, lasciviousness... they which do such things shall not inherit the kingdom of God.” Confession brings these things into the light where God's mercy can wash them away.

If we confess our sins, He is faithful and just to forgive us our sins and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness. Simply ask, and His grace is sufficient for you.

Repentance is a U-turn. It means changing your mind and your direction. Turn away from the darkness and walk toward the marvelous light of Christ.

Following Jesus requires letting go of the weights that hold us back. True discipleship is putting God first above all sinful habits and old desires.

You were not meant to walk alone. Finding a community of believers will strengthen your faith and help you grow in the knowledge of our Lord.
